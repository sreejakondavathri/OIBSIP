Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, he has made significant contributions to Indian cricket as a player and captain. Known for his aggressive batting style, unmatched consistency, and exceptional leadership qualities, he has become an inspiration for millions around the world.

As a former captain of the Indian cricket team, Kohli has led India to numerous victories in Test matches, ODIs, and T20s. With a passion for fitness, discipline, and perfection, Virat has broken countless records and continues to be a role model for aspiring cricketers worldwide.
